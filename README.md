📑 Research Index
01. Cross-Asset Moving Average (MA) Optimization
- Objective: To address the lagging nature of traditional MA models and find a "Golden Parameter" robust across different asset classes.

- Asset Classes: Equities (SPY), Cryptocurrency (BTC-USD).

- Key Methodology: Grid Search Optimization, MDD Sensitivity Analysis, and Look-ahead Bias mitigation.

- Core Achievements:

    - Identified the 10/100-day crossover as the optimal parameter for both SPY and BTC.

    - Risk Mitigation: Reduced SPY Maximum Drawdown (MDD) from -33.7% to -20.2% during the 2020 crash.

    - Alpha Generation: Achieved a cumulative return of 115.47x on Bitcoin, nearly doubling the 'Buy & Hold' benchmark (62.87x).

    - Efficiency: Validated the strategy with a Sharpe Ratio of 0.64, proving sustainable risk-adjusted returns.

- Status: Completed (Baseline for Algorithmic Trading Bot).
